About

Enthusiastic physicist applying science to literally all my endeavours.

Applied researcher with two years of experience in building an exceptional foundation of knowledge and relationships in the radiation effects community. Versed in collaborating effectively with cross-functional colleagues on internal and external teams.

An adaptable and responsible recent graduate, having an open mind and an intense desire to know more by each passing day. Particularly eager to take on the challenges of building a career and apply my knowledge both outside and within the boundaries of physics academia.
